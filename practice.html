EMPLOYMENT LAW

  • Our advice extends to the applicability of various HR Laws in India, both Central and State, including the Industrial Disputes Act, the Employees' Provident Fund Act, Trade Unions Act, Payment of Bonus Act, Payment of Gratuity Act, Contract Labour Regulations, the state-specific Shops & Establishments enactments, etc. to name a few.
  • We also advise our clients on applicability of HR Laws, the obligations and compliances thereunder, and the consequences of non-compliance, issues related to provident fund contributions, payment of gratuity, payment of bonus, superannuation/retirement benefits, termination of employment, hours of work, leave/ holiday entitlement, employee health and safety regulations, discrimination and harassment issues, etc. A key concern of structuring employee benefits and incentives is the tax implications on both the employee and the employer. We advise our clients on the structuring of a tax-efficient compensation package.

SECURITIES LAW

  • The firm handles day-to-day advisory work with regard to dynamic policies and rules amended by the Securities Exchange Board of India, which are important from a listed company’s perspective.
  • The firm also handles statutory and regulatory compliances including issues concerning Quarterly / Half Yearly / Annual Compliances under the Securities Exchange Board of India (Listing and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015 with stock exchanges and other authorities.
  • The firm also has an active securities litigation practice before the SEBI and the Securities Appellate Tribunal in defending regulatory and disciplinary action by SEBI, and in appeals before the Hon’ble High Courts and the Hon’ble Supreme Court of India.

PROJECT FINANCE

  • Project finance has evolved as the tool for evolving need for financing of projects on stand-alone basis, where the revenue stream is certain over a period of time. Project finance practice area being document intensive, the firm’s project finance practice group focuses on diligent drafting and negotiation of all the agreements so as to mitigate the risks and protect the interest of all the stakeholders.
  • The documentation involved in the project finance practice can be broadly categorized as (a) Project documents which are the documents for design, construction, operation and maintenance of a project such as incorporation documents of a special purpose vehicle (SPV), investment agreement, project management agreement, sponsor or JV agreement etc.; and (b) Financing/Security documents which are the documents for financing and securing a project which involves drafting, vetting and negotiation of security agreement, pledge agreement, etc.
  • Firm’s project finance practice group is present in various Infrastructure sectors such as Transport (Roads & Highways), Municipal Infrastructure (including Water & Sanitation services) etc. In these areas, we have been engaged in advising Project developers, Investors and Suppliers & Contractors on diverse commercial / transactional issues.

DISPUTE RESOLUTION & ARBITRATION

  • We have the expertise in handling litigations before the Hon’ble Supreme Court of India and the Hon’ble High Courts, NCDRC, State and District Consumer Forums, Labour Commissioner, District Labour Court, Competition Appellate Tribunal (CAT), CERC, SERCs, Appellate Tribunal for Electricity (APTEL), District Courts, Debt Recovery Tribunal, DRAT and various other tribunals on behalf of a diversified client base ranging from individuals to multi-national companies.
  • We formulate innovative dispute resolution methods and strategies to take recourse to legal remedies which deliver the desired results most effectively as well as efficaciously. We have handled matters pertaining to diverse areas of law across various forums and in different jurisdictions. We extensively handle, corporate litigation, commercial disputes, licensing, property related disputes, civil matters, criminal matters etc.
  • We have experienced hands working on various arbitration proceedings institutional or otherwise, involving documentation, research, drafting and briefing senior counsels in disputes inter alia concerning commercial contracts, work orders, electricity generation, distribution and trading companies. We also have experience in handling ancillary litigation in arbitration disputes arising before the Supreme Court and High Courts on behalf of a diversified client base ranging from individuals to multi-national companies.
  • We welcome and adopt alternative dispute resolution techniques in resolving a dispute wherever deemed fit and expedient and ensure our client the most effective and efficacious remedy.
